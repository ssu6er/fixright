Warranty Information

Warranty terms depend on the appliance, fault and parts used.

FixRight provides warranty support for completed repair work according to the repair type and installed components.

What the warranty covers

  • Repair work completed by our technician.
  • Installed replacement parts, according to supplier conditions.
  • The same fault confirmed during the warranty period.

What is not covered

  • New unrelated faults.
  • Damage caused by misuse, flooding, electrical surges or third-party repair attempts.
  • Normal wear and tear or consumable parts when not included in the repair.

How to request warranty support

Contact us with your name, service date, appliance model and a short description of the issue. We will review the case and arrange the next steps.
