Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026

These Terms describe the general rules for ordering appliance repair services from FixRight.

Service requests

A booking request does not guarantee immediate availability. We confirm the appointment time by phone, email or message.

Diagnostics and estimates

The technician inspects the appliance and provides an estimate before starting repair work. The customer may accept or decline the proposed repair.

Customer responsibilities

  • Provide accurate contact and address details.
  • Ensure safe access to the appliance.
  • Inform us about previous repairs, visible damage or error codes.

Payments

Payment terms are confirmed before the repair. Additional parts or work are discussed with the customer before being added.

Limitations

Some appliances may be impossible or uneconomical to repair. In such cases, we provide recommendations based on the technician’s diagnosis.
