Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FixRight collects, uses and protects personal information submitted through our website or during service communication.

Information we collect

  • Name and contact details provided in the booking form.
  • Phone number, email address and service address.
  • Description of the appliance issue and service history.

How we use information

We use this information to contact customers, schedule technician visits, prepare estimates, complete repairs, provide warranty support and improve service quality.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with technicians, service partners or legal authorities when necessary to provide the service or comply with the law.

Data retention

We keep service records only for as long as needed for customer support, accounting, warranty and legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at kontakt@fixright.pl.
